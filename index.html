What is MOV/RE?

Movie Recommendations

MOV/RE recommends movies to its users based on what they want to watch or what they watched before.

Your Feedback

MOV/RE gets feedback from its user regarding our recommendations and movies. Such, our users can get recommendations not only from MOV/RE, but from other users' comments and rated points, too!

Responsive Design

MOV/RE is a user friendly and a responsive website. Features such as search filters, user comments and rating systems allow our users to have a better experience.
